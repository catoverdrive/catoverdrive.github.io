Getting There

The Institute of Contemporary Art is located at 25 Harbor Shore Drive, Boston, MA.

Public Transit

The ICA is approximately 5 minutes away from the Courthouse stop on the Silver Line. From South Station, the SL1, SL2, SL3, and SLW will stop at Courthouse. SL4 and SL5 go in the other direction and will not get you there. From the airport, take the SL1 (the only one that stops at the airport).

Driving and Parking

There is a parking lot across the street from the ICA, and it costs $17 after 5pm. We do, however, strongly encourage you to find alternative transportation.

Lodging

We will have a block of rooms at the Boston Seaport Hotel. We'll post details once they've been finalized. The hotel is at the third-floor exit of the World Trade Center stop on the Silver Line (SL1, SL2, and SL3). It is a five to seven minute walk from the ICA, although we will likely be running shuttles to and from the venue.
