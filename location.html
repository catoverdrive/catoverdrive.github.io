Getting There

The Institute of Contemporary Art is located at 25 Harbor Shore Drive, Boston, MA.

Public Transit

The ICA is approximately 5 minutes away from the Courthouse stop on the Silver Line. From South Station, the SL1, SL2, SL3, and SLW will stop at Courthouse. SL4 and SL5 go in the other direction and will not get you there. From the airport, take the SL1 (the only one that stops at the airport).

Driving and Parking

There is a parking lot across the street from the ICA, and it costs $17 after 5pm. We do, however, strongly encourage you to find alternative transportation.

Lodging

We have a block of rooms at the Boston Seaport Hotel for $229 a night. In order to make a reservation, please call the hotel at 1-877-SEAPORT and request the group rate for the Wang & Myers Wedding Room Block.

The last day to make a reservation with the group discount is July 6th. As a heads up, we pretty strongly suggest that you book a hotel room sooner rather than later; Boston Comic Con is happening that weekend right down the street, and we expect that hotels may book up as the date gets closer.

The hotel is at the third-floor exit of the World Trade Center stop on the Silver Line (SL1, SL2, and SL3). It is a five to seven minute walk from the ICA, although we will likely be running shuttles to and from the venue.
